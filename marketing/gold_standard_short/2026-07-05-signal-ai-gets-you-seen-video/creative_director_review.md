# Signal Image-To-Video Review Packet

## Concept

Turn the provided "SIGNAL - THE AI THAT GETS YOU SEEN" image into a horizontal brand-motion video.

## Storyboard

1. Hero image opens with Signal already present in the brand system.
2. Left panel is spotlighted: Signal checks formatting, keywords, experience, and relevance.
3. Center panel is spotlighted: keyword/proof particles merge into the resume.
4. Right panel is spotlighted: the cleaner resume story lands on the hiring manager screen.
5. End card: Signal gets the resume seen, with the free Signal score CTA.

## Animation Notes

- Reference image remains the visual base so the finished video matches the art direction exactly.
- Animated cyan/green path lines travel across all three panels.
- Signal mascot moves panel-to-panel as the guide.
- Panel borders pulse to show the active step.
- Hiring manager screen gets a scan-line and glow pass.
- Captions are scene-level, not transcript fragments, to keep the brand spot polished.
