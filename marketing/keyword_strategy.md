# Keyword & Content Strategy: ATS Hacker

The focus of this keyword strategy is to capture bottom-of-the-funnel (transactional) searchers and middle-of-the-funnel (pain-point) searchers with zero ad spend. We optimize for conversions ($5 purchases) over sheer vanity traffic.

## 1. High-Intent / Transactional Keywords (Primary Focus)
These users know what an ATS is and are actively looking for a tool to solve their problem. These should be routed to our homepage or programmatic SEO pages.

*   **"Fix my resume for ATS"**
*   **"ATS resume optimizer"**
*   **"Beat Workday ATS"**
*   **"Beat Greenhouse ATS"**
*   **"Rewrite resume to match job description"**
*   **"ATS keyword scanner"**
*   **"AI resume tailored to job description"**

## 2. Pain-Point / Educational Keywords (Blog & Content Focus)
These users are frustrated but may not know *why* they are being rejected. These keywords are perfect for Reddit posts, short-form video hooks, and future blog posts.

*   **"Why is my resume not getting interviews?"**
    *   *Angle:* Explain that human recruiters never even see it. It's the robot rejecting them.
*   **"Do ATS systems read PDFs?"**
    *   *Angle:* Yes, but only if they are text-based. Complex multi-column PDFs fail parsing.
*   **"How many keywords does my resume need?"**
    *   *Angle:* Explain semantic matching vs. keyword stuffing.
*   **"Why am I getting auto-rejected in 5 minutes?"**
    *   *Angle:* The ATS filter threshold was set too high, and their semantic match score failed.

## 3. Competitor-Adjacent Keywords
People searching for alternatives to bloated, expensive subscriptions.

*   **"Jobscan alternative free"** (We are $5 one-off, which appeals to free-seekers)
*   **"Tealhq vs ResumeWorded"**
*   **"Cheapest ATS resume scanner"**

## 4. Execution Strategy
1. **Programmatic Landing Pages:** The 50 role-specific pages (e.g., "Software Engineer resume keywords") will capture the long-tail searches.
2. **Reddit/Quora Seeding:** We will search Reddit for people asking "Why is my resume not getting interviews?" and provide the educational answer from Bucket 2, disclosing ATS Hacker.
3. **YouTube SEO:** Videos titled "Beat Workday ATS (2026)" ranking on YouTube search, linking to the tool.
