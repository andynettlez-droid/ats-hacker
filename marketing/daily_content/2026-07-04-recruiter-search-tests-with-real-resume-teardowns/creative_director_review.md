## External Benchmarks

- TikTok creative guidance: first 3 seconds must establish the content proposition; first 6 seconds should drive watch time through suspense, surprise, or emotion; captions and overlays should add context.
- TikTok creative code: hook/body/close structure, faster scene changes, restrained but intentional sound, vertical safe-zone compliance, and frequent creative refreshes.
- Resume niche pattern: recruiter authority plus a visible resume artifact performs better for this product than generic AI demo videos. The viewer should feel like a recruiter caught a costly mistake.
