# Signal ATS Filter Breakthrough Draft

## Title

## Core Idea
Signal is the atomic mascot from the logo concept. It moves through a dark company-filter pathway, breaks through three soft-glowing barriers, upgrades the resume with honest role-language proof, then phases the resume into a hiring manager's computer screen.

## Audio Direction
Use quiet, premium, on-theme music only. The bed should feel like low orbit, soft synth, and confident forward motion. Avoid harsh swooshes, dings, glitch hits, alert sounds, or cartoon impacts. Sound should support the visual story, not call attention to itself.
