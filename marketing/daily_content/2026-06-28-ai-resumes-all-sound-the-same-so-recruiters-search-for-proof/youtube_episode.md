## Script And Storyboard

### Cold open

Script: This resume looks clean, polite, and professional, which is exactly the trap. The open loop today is simple: this candidate is probably qualified, but the resume is invisible because the proof is wearing camouflage.

Visual: Start on a clean resume, hard zoom to a 34/100 badge, Signal mascot points at the missing proof, then split screen with the job description.

### Stakes

Script: The painful mistake is thinking pretty language equals searchable language. A recruiter does not search for professional fog. They search for a title, tools, outcomes, and proof that matches the job description.

Visual: Show a recruiter search box typing Demand Generation Manager, HubSpot, CAC, LinkedIn Ads, lifecycle marketing, and pipeline.

### Search test

Script: For this role, the job description is screaming HubSpot, CAC analysis, LinkedIn Ads, lifecycle marketing, demand generation, pipeline, and measurable campaign performance. Those are the signals we need to find on the resume.

Visual: Highlight keyword chips from the job description and move them into a recruiter search board.

### Resume crime scene

Script: Now look at the resume. It says results-driven team player, helped with marketing campaigns, and worked with cross-functional teams. That is not bad experience. That is invisible experience.

Visual: Circle each vague bullet with red/yellow annotations and label the page qualified but hard to find.

### Why it gets missed

Script: Hiring teams are not mind readers. If the real work involved HubSpot, LinkedIn Ads, CAC, lifecycle marketing, or pipeline, but the resume hides all of it behind helped with campaigns, the evidence is wearing camouflage.

Visual: Animate the good proof fading under vague words, then Signal pulls the terms back into view.

### Fix the bullet

Script: The fix is not inventing experience. The fix is translation. Instead of helped with marketing campaigns, write: cut CAC by 32% through LinkedIn Ads audience segmentation and HubSpot lead scoring.

Visual: Type the before bullet, delete it, and replace it with the proof bullet while Signal validates each term.

### Score reveal

Script: Same person. Same work. Completely different signal. The example moves from 34 out of 100 to 92 out of 100 because the resume finally says the tools, metric, and outcome the role asked for.

Visual: Score meter jumps from 34/100 to 92/100, with green checks on HubSpot, CAC, LinkedIn Ads, lifecycle marketing, and pipeline.

### Trust check

Script: Here is the line we do not cross: no fake experience, no keyword stuffing, no ATS mythology, and no outcome promises. The resume should become clearer, not fictional.

Visual: Show four trust badges: no fake experience, no keyword stuffing, no guarantees, free score first.
