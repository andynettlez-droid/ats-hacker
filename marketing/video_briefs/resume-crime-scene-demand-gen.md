# Resume Crime Scene - Demand Gen Teardown

## Title

This Resume Got a 34/100. Here's Why.

## Format

Recruiter-reacts / resume teardown short.

Length: 35-45 seconds.

Primary visual: Resume and job description side by side.

Optional visual: HeyGen recruiter face-cam bubble for the first hook only.

## Script

This resume looks fine, which is exactly the problem. The job description asks for Demand Gen, LinkedIn Ads, HubSpot, and CAC analysis. But this resume says "responsible for social media" and "helped with campaigns."

That is not bad experience. It is invisible experience.

So we do not fake anything. We translate what they actually did.

Instead of "helped with campaigns," write: "Cut CAC by 32% through LinkedIn Ads audience segmentation and HubSpot lead scoring."

Same person. Same experience. Better signal.

Before: 34 out of 100. After: 92 out of 100.

Paste the job description and check your free Signal score before you apply.
